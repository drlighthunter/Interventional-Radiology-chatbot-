# IR Patient Guide - AI Medical Assistant

## Overview
The **IR Patient Guide** is an intelligent, multilingual AI medical assistant designed specifically to help patients understand Interventional Radiology (IR) procedures. It bridges the communication gap between complex medical terminology and patient comprehension, offering a reassuring, conversational, and highly informative experience.

## Intent
Interventional Radiology involves minimally invasive, image-guided procedures that are often less risky and have faster recovery times than traditional surgery. However, patients are frequently unaware of these options or find the procedures intimidating. 

The intent of this application is to:
- **Educate patients** about IR procedures in simple, easy-to-understand language.
- **Provide multilingual support** to ensure accessibility across different regions, particularly in India.
- **Assist with administrative hurdles**, such as insurance justifications and understanding costs.
- **Empower patients** to make informed decisions about their healthcare alongside their doctors.

## Key Features

### 🏥 Evidence-Based Clinical Guidance
The AI's knowledge base is strictly aligned with the world's leading Interventional Radiology societies and national health standards:
- **CIRSE** (Cardiovascular and Interventional Radiological Society of Europe)
- **SIR** (Society of Interventional Radiology, USA)
- **ICMR** (Indian Council of Medical Research) Standard Treatment Workflows (STWs)

### 💰 Ayushman Bharat Integration & Pricing
For Indian patients, the assistant can provide tentative pricing and information on procedures supported by **Ayushman Bharat** (PM-JAY) and state-specific portals like the Karnataka SAST Arogya portal.

### 📄 Insurance & Claim Assistance
Navigating medical insurance can be daunting. The app includes built-in capabilities to generate:
- **Insurance Premium Authorizations** (Pre-authorization requests)
- **Formal Claim Letters** and Justification Letters
*These drafts use the patient's demographics and medical history to explain why an IR procedure is medically necessary and cost-effective compared to traditional surgery.*

### 🌍 Multilingual Voice & Text Support
- Supports 10 languages including English, Hindi, Kannada, Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, Oriya, Bangla, Marathi, and Gujarati.
- Features **Text-to-Speech (TTS)** capabilities, allowing patients to listen to the medical advice in their native language.

### 📍 Location & Provider Search
- Integrated with OpenStreetMap (OSM) to help patients find nearby hospitals, clinics, and Interventional Radiology providers based on their current location.

### 🔬 Advanced Medical Tools
- **OpenFDA Integration**: Checks for drug warnings, side effects, and bleeding risks.
- **PubMed Integration**: Fetches recent medical research articles and abstracts.
- **Visual Aids**: Fetches medical diagrams and images of procedures to help patients visualize their treatment.
